"I watched the heavy front door and let out a quiet sigh... yet through the storm of doubts, a flame remains that nobody can control."

Full Studio Album • Released June 2026

the fall & the flight

The 12-movement flagship album capturing Caliptra's complete arc. From the quiet ache of velvet hinge and candle in the foyer to the soaring devotion of where your hand fits in and beautiful life, this record bridges absolute duality and spiritual rebirth.

✨ Highlighted Key Tracks
Album Finale

1. beautiful life

"Alhamdulillah for this beautiful life... for the valleys and the peaks, through the ease and through the strife."

A triumphant anthem of gratitude and spiritual rebirth that closes the album on an unshakeable note of inner peace. It celebrates finding grounded strength through life’s sharpest trials and blessings alike.

Opening Track

2. velvet hinge

"And it’s a velvet hinge on a heavy door, swinging shut on the life we were headed for."

An intimate acoustic opener capturing the delicate, quiet moment of stepping away from a past relationship. It sets the emotional tone of the record over cold coffee, rain, and heavy velvet doors.

Key Movement

3. counterfeit forever

"A counterfeit of a forever we couldn't keep together... I painted all the gray into gold."

A cinematic ballad exploring the lingering ache of unkept promises and vanishing acts. It portrays the struggle of building kingdoms in the dark while trying to transform grief into beauty.
